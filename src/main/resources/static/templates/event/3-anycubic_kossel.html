tuning

you will need to tune 3 things: height, extrusion length, stepper drivers

height

adjust your endstops to the same height.
home and move down until you can just fit a piece paper under the nozzle
adapt the height in the firmware to your z value at that position
for example if the z of the lowest position is -2.4 add 2.4 to your MANUAL_Z_HOME in the firmware

extrusion length

measure & mark x cm of your filament and extrude x cm from your preferred printer client software
measure and check if marked filament moved x cm
adapt the steps per mm in your firmware if needed and repeat

stepper drivers

normally they should work in between .4 - .6 V pretty well
just don't let your steppers get too hot
you shouldn't burn your hand touching them

for measuring you should connect your multimeter's + to the voltage regulator and the ground pin to your multimeter's -
adjust and measure again


slicer settings

print speed: 15-30mm/s (depending on wanted quality)
layer height: 0.1mm
top/bottom/wall speed: 15mm/s
inner wall speed: 30mm/s
outer wall speed: 15 mm/s
travel speed: 30-60 mm/s (depending on wanted quality)
retraction distance: 4.2mm
alternating skin rotation: true
adaptive layers: true

if support:
pattern: triangles
towers: true
