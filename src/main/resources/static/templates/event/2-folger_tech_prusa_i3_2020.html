tuning

you'll need to tune at least the stepper drivers i recommend leveling by a sensor, since i had a lot of problems with everything vibrating, screws moving and thus the heat plate and printing surface leaving its position

stepper drivers

should work in between .4 - .6 V pretty well

for measuring connect the potentiometer to + and the ground pin to - of your voltmeter adjust and measure again
